About

Hi, I'm Dominic and currently work as a Graduate Data Scientist at Admiral Group.


Academic Background

- BSc Mathematics & Economics (Durham University) (1st, 76%). Albert Green Prize for highest attainment in Mathematics.
- MSc Statistics (University of St Andrews) (Distinction, 18.0 GPA). Royal Statistical Society Prize for highest attainment.

Experience

I have experience in (Household) Risk and Retail Pricing in the Insurance industry:
- Created new rating score for Price Elasticity to identify low and high elasticity groups and optimize pricing structure accordingly to maximize profit within regulatory and moral constraints.
- Extracted value from Environment Agency Flood Data API to improve pricing of surface water run-off and automate claim alerts.
- Manual rate changes to current pricing structure.
- Developed Perils-based pricing structure tuned to individual perils (Accidental Damage, Escape of Water, Fire, Theft, Flood, etc.) to improve pricing granularity, transparency and ability to react to peril-specific changes in risk. Techniques centred around GLMs, GAMs and Gradient-Boosted Ensembles.


Skills

  • Python, R, MATLAB, SQL, SAS
  • Parametric Supervised Learning: GLMs, GAMs, Splines, Mixed Effects Models, Regularization (LASSO, Elastic Net)
  • Non-Parametric Supervised Learning: Random Forest, XGBoost, SVM
  • Unsupervised Learning: PCA, Cluster Analysis, Mixture Modelling
  • Deep Learning: Neural Networks
  • Numerical Optimization (Simulated Aneeling, Expectation-Maximization)
  • Bayesian Machine Learning: Bayesian A/B Testing, Variable Selection (Horseshoe, Spike-Slab Priors)
  • Google Cloud Platform (BigQuery, Kubeflow, Looker, Azure Devops & GIT)
